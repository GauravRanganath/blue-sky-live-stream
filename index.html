Viridian


proper noun

(1) Blue Sky Solar Racing's 10th Generation car.


noun

(1) a bluish-green pigment.


Viridian was chosen as the Gen X solar car name for what it represents to the team. To pay homage to the past cars the team has built, Viridian is a shade of blue, but it is a blue-green colour. It shows evolution from the first solar car, Blue, and a deviation from the norm.
